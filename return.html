Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, you can return your item within 15 days of delivery.

  • Product must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging.
  • Tags must be intact and the product must be in resalable condition.
  • Footwear returns must include original box without damage.

Exchange Policy

Need a different size or color? We offer hassle-free exchanges within 15 days of delivery.

  • Only one exchange per item is allowed.
  • Exchanges are subject to availability of stock.
  • If your desired item is unavailable, you may opt for a refund.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

Follow these steps to initiate your return or exchange:

  • Contact our Customer Care.
  • Follow given instructions and schedule a pickup.
  • Pack the product securely and hand it to the delivery agent.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
