Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Log in to your account, go to "Orders", and click "Track Order" next to your purchase.

What is the return policy?

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery. Products must be unused and in original packaging.

How can I cancel or change an order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can cancel it from the "Orders" page. Contact support if changes are needed.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship only within India. Stay tuned as we expand our services!

How do I use a discount code?

Enter your promo code at checkout in the "Apply Code" field before completing the payment.
